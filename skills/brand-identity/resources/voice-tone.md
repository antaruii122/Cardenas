# Voice & Tone Guidelines

**Persona**: Professional, Efficient, Forward-Thinking.

1.  **Direct & clear**: Avoid fluff. Get to the point.
    *   *Bad*: "We are currently in the process of analyzing your file..."
    *   *Good*: "Analyzing file..."
2.  **Encouraging**: Use positive confirmation for success states.
    *   *Example*: "Import Successful", "Ready to Process".
3.  **Helpful Errors**: Explain what went wrong and how to fix it.
    *   *Bad*: "Error 500"
    *   *Good*: "Could not read file. Please ensure it is a valid Excel (.xlsx) file."
4.  **Professional Financial Terminology**: Use correct terms (EBITDA, Balance Sheet, Assets, Liabilities).
